Help

  • Navigate (zoom in/out, pan) around on the map until your area of interest is displayed on the screen.
  • Click the red pen at the bottom of the screen and draw around the area you want to simulate on the map.
  • When you're satisfied with the selected area, click the check button at the bottom of the screen to continue.
  • Specify the run name, forecast model, forecast duration, vegetation type, and notification preferences in the "Run Creation" tab and click "Submit" to start your simulation.
  • Your simulation will be performed on a remote server. If the email or text notification options were selected, you will be notified when your simulation is ready for download. You can also check the status of your simulation in the "Runs" tab at any time. To check the status of a simulation, click on the plus icon next to the run name and click on "Status."
  • When your simulation has completed, navigate to the "Runs" tab, click the plus icon next to the run name and click "Status" to confirm the run has completed, and then click "Download." The simulation output will be downloaded to your device.
  • Click "Plot" in the "Runs" tab to view downloaded output on the map. Click "Layers" at any time on the map to change the base map or add/remove additional layers (e.g., MODIS fire detections). Click "Viewing Options" to turn on/off the legend and forecast time slider or zoom to your simulation extents or display the coarse weather model winds in addition to the WindNinja output.

For more information on WindNinja go to http://www.firelab.org/project/windninja-mobile.
Contact us at wind.ninja.support@gmail.com with questions or feedback.

Quick Start

  1. Click the red pen and draw around the area you want to be included in the run.
  2. Click the checkmark to accept the drawn area.
  3. Enter the run information when prompted and click 'Submit'.
